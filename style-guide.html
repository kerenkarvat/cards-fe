Design Principles

✨ Sophisticated Magic

Create wonder through subtle interactions and refined visual elements. Magic that feels premium and professional.

🔮 Glass Morphism

Embrace translucent, blurred backgrounds with subtle borders. Create depth through layering and backdrop filters.

🌟 Gentle Hierarchy

Guide users naturally through content with soft shadows, gentle elevations, and clear visual hierarchy.

💫 Accessible Magic

Ensure sufficient contrast, readable fonts, and intuitive interactions. Magic should be inclusive.

🎨 Cohesive Experience

Maintain consistent spacing (8px grid), border-radius (12px+), and animation timing (0.2s ease).

⭐ Premium Feel

High-quality gradients, smooth transitions, and thoughtful micro-interactions create a premium experience.
